The "First Mover" Advantage

Our strategy is structured around the supply deficit window. Selling when the market is empty.

Accelerated Market Entry

The northern regions (Quba-Khachmaz) are the main producers, but they harvest 2-3 weeks later. We enter the market in May-June when supply is structurally low and prices are at their peak (60-80% premium).

900+ Chill Hours

Unlike lowland zones (Kurdamir), our foothills guarantee trees a full winter dormancy. Critical for consistent flowering.

UV Impact

Elevation provides 15% more UV radiation. Cherries achieve a premium "Dark" color. The visual appeal drives export demand.

Calcium Structure

Carbonate soils act as natural cement. The fruit texture becomes dense and crunchy, allowing safe long-haul transport.

No Logistics Burden

Sales are executed through pre-qualified export partners (FCA Model). Buyers handle packing and transport. Pre-payment on the field.

Realistic Biological Timeline

Planting in Q4 2026. Rooting in 2027. First commercial harvest in 2029 (Year 3). Conservative modeling eliminates unmet expectations.
