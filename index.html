The "First Mover" Advantage

Our strategy is simple: Harvest when the market is empty. Sell before the price drops.

Beating Quba & Khachmaz

The northern regions (Quba-Khachmaz) are the main producers, but they harvest 2-3 weeks later than Ismayilli. We enter the market in May-June when supply is low and prices are at their peak. By the time the "big wave" from the north arrives, we have already sold our crop.

900+ Chill Hours

Unlike lowland zones (Kurdamir, Sabirabad), our foothills guarantee trees a full winter dormancy. This is a critical factor for consistent flowering and crop formation.

UV Impact & Coloration

Elevation provides 15% more UV radiation. Cherries achieve a premium "Dark" color, and Giant Red Apricots become deep red. The visual appeal sells the product.

Calcium Structure

Carbonate soils act as natural cement for the fruit. The texture becomes dense and crunchy. This allows long-haul transport to Moscow or Dubai.

Zero Logistics Headache

We do not chase buyers. Exporters contract the harvest directly at the orchard gate (FCA Model). Pre-payment on the field.

Biological Reality

We prioritize truth over hype. Planting in Q4 2026. Rooting in 2027. First commercial harvest in 2029 (Year 3). This conservative modeling eliminates the risk of unmet expectations.
