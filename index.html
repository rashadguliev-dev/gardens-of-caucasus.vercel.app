Fundamental Advantage

Why Ismayilli (650m ASL)? It's not just land, it's a climate hedge for your investment.

900+ Chill Hours

Unlike lowland zones (Kurdamir, Sabirabad), our foothills guarantee trees a full winter dormancy. This is a critical factor for consistent flowering and crop formation. Without this, the orchard risks becoming just "firewood".

UV Impact & Coloration

Elevation provides 15% more UV radiation. Cherries achieve a premium "Dark" color, and Giant Red Apricots become deep red. The visual appeal sells the product.

Calcium Structure

Carbonate soils act as natural cement for the fruit. The texture becomes dense and crunchy. This allows long-haul transport to Moscow or Dubai without quality loss.

Biological Reality

We prioritize truth over hype. Planting in Q4 2026. Rooting in 2027. First commercial harvest in 2029 (Year 3). This conservative modeling eliminates the risk of unmet expectations.
