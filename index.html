Strategic Market Opportunity

Capitalizing on structural supply deficits in the May-June window.

Earlier Market Entry

The northern regions (Quba-Khachmaz) harvest 2-3 weeks later than Ismayilli. We enter the market in May-June during the deficit window when pricing commands a 60-80% premium. By the time the "big wave" arrives, our crop is sold.

UV Impact & Coloration

Elevation provides 15% more UV radiation. Cherries achieve a premium "Dark" color, and Giant Red Apricots become deep red. Visual appeal drives export demand.

No Logistics Burden

Sales are executed through pre-qualified export partners under contractual purchase arrangements (FCA Model). Pre-payment on the field is standard.

Realistic Biological Timeline

We prioritize truth over hype. Planting in Q4 2026. Rooting in 2027. First commercial harvest in 2029 (Year 3). This conservative modelling eliminates the risk of unmet expectations.

Calcium Structure

Carbonate soils act as natural cement for the fruit. The texture becomes dense and crunchy, allowing for safe long-haul transport.
